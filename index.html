About Us


At Developing Minds Psychological Services Ltd., Dr. Campbell Reay leads a team of Chartered Clinical Neuropsychologists, and Associate Professionals with a wealth of experience. Their services, based in the Midlands are available nationwide, provide support for individuals, couples, families, case managers, solicitors and organisations such as the National Health Service, Social Services, and the criminal justice system.

For more details, please contact us with any questions you may have. Please be assured all contacts will be handled in the utmost confidence.
