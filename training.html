Training


We believe that well trained staff are more likely to be confident and achieve their full potential, resulting in a more effective and efficient organisation. Developing Minds training can be tailored specifically to your needs. Carefully planned training interventions can both pre-empt problems and contribute significantly to the development of expertise.

Some of the previous recipients of our training have included:

  • NHS multi-professional groups
  • Social Services multi-professional groups
  • Police officers and civilian staff
  • Traumatic Brain Injury Case managers
  • Support Workers working with Traumatic Brain Injury
  • Doctorate in Clinical Psychology Trainees
  • Solicitors and Associate Solicitors

Here are some examples of training we have previously delivered:

  • Understanding the Neuropsychology of Traumatic Brain Injury and Neurodegenerative conditions
  • How to Assess Mental Capacity
  • Mental health awareness training
  • Understanding and working with different types of Dementia
  • Understanding and Managing Depression
  • Coping with Stress
  • Understanding and Managing Anxiety
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder
  • Developing health and wellbeing approaches
  • Working with Bereavement and Loss
  • Managing challenging behaviours
  • Assertiveness skills
  • Developing listening and communication skills
