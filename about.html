About Developing Minds


Dr Alice Campbell Reay holds a B.Sc.(Hons) in Psychology; M.Sc. in Clinical Psychology and a Ph.D.. She is a Chartered Consultant Clinical Neuropsychologist and Forensic Psychologist.
Alice is a member of the Specialist Register of Clinical Neuropsychologists with the British Psychological Society (SRCN) both and a Full Member of the Division of Clinical Psychology.
Alice is a Registered Practitioner Psychologist in Clinical and Forensic Psychologist with the Health Care Professions Council.
Alice is a Fellow of the Royal Society of Medicine, an Associate Fellow of the British Psychological Society and a Chartered Scientist. She is a member of the International Neuropsychology Society and National Academy of Neuropsychology (NAN, USA) She is also registered on the British Psychological Society Register of Applied Psychology Practice Supervisors (RAPPS). Alice has previously held honorary lectureships at the University of Glasgow and the University of Sheffield and has been an External Examiner for the Doctorate in Clinical Psychology at the University of Exeter.
Alice is an Honorary Lecturer at the University of Nottingham and has published widely in the field of Elder Abuse, Neglect and Mental Capacity.
Alice has also been a Member on British Psychological Society working parties on the Law Commission’s Unfitness to Plead Guidance; Psychologists as Expert Witnesses; and Mental Capacity. She has been a Trustee of Action for Elder Abuse and Headway Derby.
Alice has presented many papers at conferences and continues to enjoy training and development.
Alice has also contributed to a number of national television and radio programmes.
Alice is an accredited and specialist Expert Advisor to the National Crime Agency, having undertaken some extremely complex assessments for a range of serious offences, assessing both victims and offenders and appearing in Crown Court as an expert witness to give evidence.
Dr Campbell Reay’s CV is available on request.


Developing Minds Psychological Services Ltd. have a team of Chartered Clinical Psychologists and other Associate Professionals who all have substantial experience and are also registered with the HCPC. Whilst based across the Midlands, our services are available nationwide. We offer a range of psychological services to individuals, couples, and families. We have also worked extensively with case managers and rehabilitation teams; organisations such as; the National Health Service; Social Services; Police Forces; care homes and the legal profession. Our website is designed to give you an overview of what we provide.
